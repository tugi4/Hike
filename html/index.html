Following the trip up to the mountain station, it is worth taking a short detour to the top of the Diedamskopf to get a breath-taking panorama. Via Kreuzle you arrive at the peak of Falzer Kopf and continue to the Neuhornbachhaus. From there, you hike across the Falzalpe and Breitenalpe as far as the middle station, without having a great difference in altitude to overcome. You arrive back in the valley in comfort – by cable car.

The Karren is a mountain within the city bounds of Dornbirn, in Western Austria. The panorama restaurant on the top of the karren. A cable car goes to the top of the mountain. The summit station includes a panorama restaurant with an expansive view of Switzerland and Germany and the Rhine Valley.
